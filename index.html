About myself

I am currently a postdoc in UC Irvine and got my PhD from Peking University. My research method widely involves Satellite data analysis, Earth System Model (ESM) modelling and statistical modelling. I'm full of enthusiasms on exploring land-atmosphere interaction via physical and biogeochemical cycles. Never think of myself as a creator but a finder of nature. Also a traveler addicted to seeing the world. CV Syl
